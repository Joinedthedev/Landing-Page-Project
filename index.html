What is Islam?

Islam is a religion that revolves around the belief that there is no God but Allah and Prophet Mohammed(peace be upon him) is His Messenger. The followers of this religon are called muslims.The teachings of islam offers guidance to its followers through the Quran and the Sunnah(actions of the Prophet(pbuh))
