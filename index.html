What is Islam?

Islam is a religion that revolves around the belief that there is no God but Allah and Prophet Mohammed(peace be upon him) is His Messenger. The followers of this religion are called muslims.The teachings of islam offers guidance to its followers through the Quran and the Sunnah(actions of the Prophet(pbuh))

The Five Pillars of Islam

Shahadah
"the Muslim profession of faith (‘there is no god but Allah, and Muhammad(ﷺ) is the messenger of Allah’).
Salah
A ritual prayer performed five times daily by muslims.
Zakat
An obligatory charity imposed on all muslims to the poor.
Fasting
Abstinence from food and drink from sunrise to sunset.
Hajj
An obligatory pilgrimage to the Holy Khaba where various rituals of worship are performed.
